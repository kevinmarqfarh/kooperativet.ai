# Code Of Conduct

Kooperativet.ai is for builders with different backgrounds, experience levels, identities and goals. Participation must be respectful, specific and safe enough for people to ask early questions.

## Expected Behavior

- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Give critique that is specific, actionable and grounded in evidence.
- Assume early work is unfinished and help improve it.
- Respect privacy, consent and boundaries.
- Credit other people's work, prompts and ideas.
- Flag problems instead of escalating conflicts in-thread.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, hate speech or discriminatory remarks.
- Sexualized comments or unwanted attention.
- Doxxing, sharing private information or pressuring people to reveal identity.
- Spam, scams, impersonation or deceptive promotion.
- Plagiarism or presenting someone else's work as your own.
- Instructions that enable abuse, illegal activity or unsafe deployment.

## Enforcement

Reports are reviewed by human moderators or administrators. Actions can include a warning, content removal, temporary restrictions, removal from specific spaces, or account suspension.

Moderation decisions should be logged with a reason, visible to the moderation team, and appealable when practical.
